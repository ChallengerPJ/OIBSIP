Indian Playback Singer

LATA MANGESHKAR

TRIBUTE PAGE

1929-2022

Lata Mangeshkar was a very popular singer. She has sung more than 30 thousand songs in 30 languages ​​in her entire lifetime. All the songs sung by her are very adorable and unique. People used to affectionately call her Lata didi, she was also known as Swar Kokila.She was honored with many National and Filmfare Awards. She was honored with the 'Padma Bhushan' and 'the Bharat Ratna' award by the Government of India. In recognition of his contribution to Indian films, she was also honored with the 'Dada Saheb Phalke Award'. Lata Mangeshkar was born on 28 September 1929 in the Indore city of Madhya Pradesh. The name of Lata Mangeshkar is recorded in the Guinness Book for singing the most number of songs in the world. She died on 6th February 2022 at Breach Candy Hospital in Mumbai.Lata Mangeshkar is one of the best singers in the world and the best female singer in India until now. She has attained a big name and fame for her work, and that is why she has sung a lot of songs and made a lot of records in her life. She has sung more than 50000 songs so far in her career. She achieved many records and received many awards for her songs. In an interview, Lata herself declared, "Ghulam Haider is truly my Godfather and the first music director for the faith in my talent."
